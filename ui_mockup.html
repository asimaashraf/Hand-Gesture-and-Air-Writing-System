How to Use

Open HandShow open palm to detect Open gesture.

PointerRaise index finger to detect Pointer gesture.

Victory / PeaceRaise index & middle finger.

Thumbs Up / DownRaise thumb for up or down.

Closed FistMake a fist to detect Closed Fist.

Select ColorMove your index finger to the color buttons above.

DrawUse your index finger to draw in the air.

Clear CanvasShow open palm to clear the canvas.

Eraser ModeSelect eraser and move finger to erase the drawing.
